Cognizant Student
Club SIST

Cognizant is an American multinational corporation that provides IT services, including digital, technology, consulting, and operations services. The Cognizant Student Club is launched to bridge the gap between the college students and the corporate and to identify best talents via continuous evaluation. Objective of Cognizant Student Club is to develop the technical knowledge and skills of the students, to identify and hire best talents via continuous evaluation, enhance the communication and interpersonal skills of the students, provide a holistic personality development, corporate and Industry Exposure.
