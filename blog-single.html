About Cognizant Student Club

Published on Feb 22, 2020

Cognizant is an American multinational corporation that provides IT services, including digital, technology, consulting and operations services

Cognizant has started spectacular range of clubs to empower Student Communities on campuses. The Cognizant Student Club is launched to bridge the gap between the college students and the corporate and to identify best talents via continuous evaluation. Cognizant believes pre final year is the right time for enhancing their skills and get prepared for placement. In this regard Cognizant student club will facilitate to develop their skills and competency.

Objective of Cognizant Student Club is to develop the technical knowledge and skills of the students, to identify and hire best talents via continuous evaluation, enhance the communication and interpersonal skills of the students, provide a holistic personality development, corporate and Industry Exposure

We are privileged to be a part of Cognizant family as a Cognizant Student Club at Sathyabama.
