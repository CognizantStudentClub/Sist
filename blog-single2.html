Beginning of the Journey

Published on Feb 12, 2020

The Cognizant club has bought numerous changes in the daily schedules of the members who are trying their best to help their college retain the top spot.

During the orientation session of this club, we were motivated to work for the following year with all the focus and strength. However, working for a club where your contributions are affecting the team as a whole requires a great deal of concentration.

Besides the academics and regular classes, we as a club are developing to prioritise our tasks according to the given situations and emergencies. Not only is it helping us shape our personalities for the future, but it is also nurturing our inner leaders.

With the immense support of our faculty members, we aspire to lead the charts with the contribution of each and every member.
